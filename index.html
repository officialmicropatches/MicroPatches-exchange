Our Mission

We are on a mission to 3D print a miniature patch keychain for every law enforcement agency, fire department, military unit, and first responder organization in the world. Every single one. Completely free. You just pay the cost of the keychain.

Our long term goal is to donate a portion of all proceeds to the families of fallen officers, veterans, and first responders.

About MicroPatches
Who We Are

MicroPatches is a new small business based out of Phoenix, Arizona, founded by an active duty law enforcement officer and United States Army National Guard veteran.

We specialize in 3D printed scaled down versions of real patches, producing keychains, magnets, and croc charms for law enforcement, military, fire, EMS, and custom designs. Everything we make is USA made, with no minimum orders, so no agency or unit gets left out regardless of size.

Our free patch queue allows anyone to submit their agency or unit and we will design and print it at no charge. You just pay for the keychain at cost when it is ready. Custom orders are handled separately, take priority, and are priced accordingly with a minimum design fee to keep the queue fair for everyone.

We also run a patch exchange program. Send us your patch and when your agency comes up in the queue, one of your keychains is on us. You just cover shipping.

Our long term mission is to donate a portion of all sales to the families of fallen first responders and military veterans.

Partner
We are proud to be partnered with the Pink Patch Project, a nationwide initiative raising awareness and funds for breast cancer research through law enforcement. We are actively seeking more partnerships that align with that mission.

We are just getting started, but we are building something we believe in.
